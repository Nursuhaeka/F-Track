How do I add a new expense? +

To add a new expense:

  1. Go to the "Add Expense" page from the sidebar
  2. Select the category for your expense
  3. Enter the amount and date
  4. Add any additional notes if needed
  5. Click "Save Expense"

Your expense will now appear in your budget reports.

How can I set a monthly budget for a category? +

Setting a monthly budget is easy:

  1. Navigate to "Set Budget" from the sidebar
  2. Select the category you want to budget for
  3. Enter your monthly budget amount
  4. Set an alert threshold if desired
  5. Click "Save Budget"

You can view your budget progress on the "List Budgets" page.

How do I change my account password? +

To change your password:

  1. Go to "Settings" in the sidebar
  2. Select the "Security" tab
  3. Click on "Change Password"
  4. Enter your current password and new password
  5. Confirm the new password and click "Save"

For security reasons, we recommend changing your password regularly.
