About This App

The Family Expense Tracker helps families manage their finances together. Track shared expenses, monitor budgets, and gain insights into your spending patterns as a household. Our mission is to make family financial management simple, transparent, and collaborative.

Key Features

Track expenses for entire family
Monthly budget planning
Expense categorization
Visual reports and analytics
Receipt scanning and storage
Voice input for expenses
Multi-user access
Customizable categories

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. All financial data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. You maintain full ownership of your data, and we never share it with third parties without your explicit consent.

Our application uses industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including secure authentication and regular security audits.
